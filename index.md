# Privacy Policy for Habit Tracker

**Last updated: November 26, 2025**

---

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

By using Habit Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Data

**Habit Tracker does NOT collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) on our servers.**

All your habit data is stored **locally on your device** and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

### 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Habit Information**: Habit names, icons, colors, reminder times, and goals
- **Habit Logs**: Daily completion records and streaks
- **App Settings**: Theme preferences, notification settings
- **In-App Purchase Status**: Whether you have purchased the premium version

This data is stored using:
- SQLite database (local storage)
- SharedPreferences (app settings)

**We do not have access to this data.**

### 3. Advertising Identifier

If you use the free version of the app (with ads), we collect your **Advertising ID** through Google AdMob to serve personalized advertisements.

The Advertising ID is a unique, user-resettable identifier provided by Google for advertising purposes.

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data Usage

Your locally stored habit data is used solely to:
- Display your habits and progress
- Calculate streaks and statistics
- Send reminder notifications
- Provide app functionality

### Advertising ID Usage

Your Advertising ID is used by Google AdMob to:
- Serve relevant advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud

**We do not use this data for any other purpose.**

---

## Third-Party Services

Habit Tracker uses the following third-party services:

### 1. Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

**Purpose**: Display advertisements

**Data Collected**: Advertising ID, device information, ad interaction data

**Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Opt-out**: You can opt-out of personalized advertising by:
- Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization (Android)
- Or by purchasing the Premium version (removes all ads)

### 2. Google Play In-App Billing

**Purpose**: Process premium purchases

**Data Collected**: Purchase transaction data (managed by Google)

**Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3. Local Notifications

**Purpose**: Send habit reminders

**Data Collected**: None (handled locally by your device)

**We do not track or collect notification data.**

---

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.**

Your habit data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

The only data shared with third parties is:
- **Advertising ID** (shared with Google AdMob for ad serving)
- **Purchase data** (shared with Google Play for transaction processing)

---

## Data Security

We take data security seriously:

- **Local Storage**: All habit data is stored locally using encrypted databases when supported by your device
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store any data on our servers
- **No Account Required**: The app does not require account creation or login

However, please note:
- **Device Security**: Your data security depends on your device's security (lock screen, encryption)
- **Backup**: If you use device backup services (Google Drive, etc.), your local data may be backed up automatically by your OS

---

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **Premium Purchase**: Linked to your Google Play account permanently
